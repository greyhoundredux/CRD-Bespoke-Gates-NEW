About Me

I'm Christopher R Danby, proud founder of CRD Bespoke Gates. I am based in the tranquil village of Honeybourne located in the North Cotswolds.

Using my fourteen years of experience, I strive to craft the best quality gates possible. As a labour of love, I ensure that the same quality and care goes in to each and every gate I make.

Have specific requirements? Not a problem. Every gate I make is tailor made to your specifications and with your satisfaction in mind. The sky is the limit here at CRD Bespoke Gates.

Iroko Hardwood

All gates can be made using Iroko hardwood originating from the tropical west coast of Africa.

Iroko is a reliable choice of hardwood owing to its durable nature that is resistant to both rot and insect attack. It's gorgeous, natural, golden brown colouring makes this wood a prime candidate for a gate you'll love.

Red Deal Softwood

Originating from Scandinavia and Russia, Red Deal softwood makes for a popular favourite owing to its longevity and beautiful pale gold aesthetic.

Red Deal is a versatile wood which makes for great driveway gates, garden gates and side-entrance gates.
